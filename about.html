Where it Started?



Geneciran Clothing Apparel

The business started year 1986, Joyce's grandmother, Gloria Gopez started a clothing apparel located in Bamban, Tarlac and it was called "Geneciran Clothing Apparel". The business first capital is only 1000 pesos. The clothes were less than 10 on her first items. The purpose of creating her own business is to gain profit from the clothes that she doesn’t wear anymore. In year 1987 the business grew and became popular in their area. The business developed and selling more than 50 items approximately. They found their own location in bamban public market. Wayback in 1998, Her grandmother decided to go to states and made Joyce's mother, Ligaya Geneciran the heir of the business. She did not change the name of the business. The reason why she retained the name because the business is flourishing and became successful.

 Why Joygene?



Joygene Merchandising

Joygene Merchandising acts as a retailer store with a variety of clothes and imported goods located at Bamban, Tarlac. The name "Joygene" came from the name of the owner. It is owned by Joyce Geneciran Maun and it started on September 2012. The reason why she chose to include "Merchandising" in the business name, so that she can sell other products aside from clothes. She get products imported from United States with the support of her mother. The items that she’s selling are t-shirts, dress, shorts, underwears, accessories, bags, baby products, imported snacks and chocolates. The goal of the business is to be popular and to help others wear fashionable clothes in an affordable price.
