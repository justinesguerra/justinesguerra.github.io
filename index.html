About Joygene Merchandising

Wear the greatest fashion today

Year 1986, Joyce's grandmother started a clothing apparel business and it was called "Geneciran clothing apparel". Wayback in 1998, Her grandmother decided to go to states and made Joyce's mother the heir of the business. She did not change the name of the business. The reason why she retained the name because the business is flourishing and became successful. In year 2012, Mrs. Joyce Geneciran Maun became the owner of the business and decided to change the name to "Joygene Merchandising".
